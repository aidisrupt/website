DDD Perth 2025 will be held at St Catherine's College at Curtin University at 433 University Bvd, Bentley WA 6102.

## Getting to the Venue

There are multiple convenient ways to reach Curtin University for AI Disrupt Summit Perth 2025:

### Public Transport

- **Train**:
    - Take the **Thornlie** line to **Oats Street Station**
    - Or take the **Mandurah line** to **Canning Bridge Station**, then transfer to a Curtin bus.

- **Bus**:
    - Curtin's **Central Bus Station** on the Bentley campus is served by multiple Transperth routes.
    - Use the Transperth website to find the fastest route (https://www.transperth.wa.gov.au/)

### Driving
    - Driving to Curtin’s Bentley campus is straightforward. It’s about **15 minutes by car from Perth CBD**, depending on traffic.
    - Parking is permitted free of charge on weekends

## On arrival
    - When you arrive, follow campus signage (or use the Curtin campus map) to find **St Catherine’s College (Building 433)**.  
    - We will be waiting inside the main entry to welcome you!
