<p>
    Josh serves as the Chair and Treasurer for DDD Perth, providing leadership and managing the financial aspects of the

## Rebecca Waters - Vice Chair

<div style={{ display: 'flex', alignItems: 'flex-start' }}>
    <img src="/images/team/rebecca-waters.jpeg" alt="Rebecca Waters" style={{ width: '150px', marginRight: '20px' }} />
</div>
<p>
    Rebecca serves as the Vice Chair of DDD Perth, bringing valuable leadership and vision to the organization. With
    extensive experience in the Perth technology community, she previously served as the Chair of DDD WA, where she
    helped grow DDD Perth into one of the largest developer conferences in Australia, attracting 1,400 tech
    professionals in 2023.
</p>
<p>
    Known for her dedication to creating inclusive and accessible tech events, Rebecca has been instrumental in shaping
    DDD Perth's culture and community impact. She combines strong organizational skills with a passion for connecting
    people across the technology sector, ensuring that DDD Perth continues to be an approachable event that appeals to
    the whole community, especially those who don't normally get to attend or speak at conferences.
</p>
<p>
    In her role, Rebecca provides strategic guidance for the organization while overseeing various operational aspects
    including ticketing and agenda coordination. Her leadership has been key to DDD Perth's continued success and growth
    as a premier technology conference in Western Australia.
</p>
